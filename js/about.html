The Mission

Human Resource Support (HRS) is a multi-dimensional platform designed to bridge the gap between creative talent and industry opportunities. We operate at the intersection of influencer marketing, digital job placement, and sustainable retail.

In a rapidly changing digital economy, our goal is to provide the infrastructure creators need to find meaningful work and for brands to find authentic voices.

What We Do

  • Influencer Marketing: Connecting bold creators with branded campaigns that matter.
  • Job Opportunities: A curated board for creative and digital-first career paths.
  • Sustainable Fashion: High-quality, limited-run drops like our UNIT series.
